Case: Devices with no Ignition events

With a properly installed device you should see Ignition ON / Ignition OFF events every time the vehicle is turned on or off.

Cause: The WHITE wire is not connected to a source that shows 12+ volts when the vehicle ignition is ON.

Solution: Make sure that the WHITE wire is connected to a line that ONLY has power when the vehicle is ON.
