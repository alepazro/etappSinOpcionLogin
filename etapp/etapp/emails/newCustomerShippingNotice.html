GETTING STARTED

Once your device is installed it will start reporting immediately. Please find below an initial introduction on how to use the system.

How to Log in

You can access the system from any computer that has Internet access. Please follow these steps:

  • Go to https://www.easitrack.net
  • You will find the Log in and Password boxes, and a button to Sign In.
  • Enter your Log in and Password and then click in Sign In.
  • After clicking Sign In you will be taken to the Mapping page. You are logged in!

How to navigate the main modules of the system

The system has 5 main modules:

  • Tracking
  • Multi tracking
  • Reports
  • Vehicle Maintenance
  • Settings

After you log in, you will be located in the Tracking module. To go from module to module just select the module in the top bar of the screen:

  • Look at the top bar of the screen, which his a dark blue bar where the name EasiTrack is located at.
  • In the center of the bar, you will see the names of the 4 modules: Tracking, Multi Tracking, Reports, Maintenance, and Settings. Each one of the module names has a circle besides it, called a Radio Button. By clicking in these circles, or Radio buttons, you will be able to navigate from one module to the other.
  • Click in the Radio Button of Reports. You will be taken to the Reports Module.
  • Now click in the Radio button of Settings. You will be taken to the Settings Module.

How to track your vehicles

Tracking your vehicles is very simple. Just navigate to the Tracking module (see above for instructions on how to navigate from module to module). Once you are in the Tracking module, the system will show a full screen map with your vehicles.

The Tracking module has a sub-menu right below the big blue bar. This sub-menu has five (5) options:

  • Fleet Location
  • Quick Dispatching
  • Breadcrumb Trail
  • Fleet Board
  • Hot Spots

Lets explain the most common options:

Fleet Location option

From this option you can have a list view of your vehicles, each one with a color-coded background that indicates the status of each vehicle. The most used color codes are:

  • GREEN: When the vehicle is moving
  • YELLOW: When the vehicle is idle
  • RED: When the vehicle is stopped.

Quick Dispatching Option

From this option you can locate a point based on an address, like a job site, or a customer site. Then you can locate the closest vehicle to it. You can even generate and send the Driving Directions to the driver.

Breadcrumb Trail Option

From this option you can see the detail route of a given vehicle in a time-frame. Just select the vehicle, enter the time frame, and that’s it! You will see in the map the route of your vehicle. You will also see a list of all the locations of the vehicle in the time frame.

How to generate a report

Go to Reports Module. From this option you can have iver 20 reports. The most common reports are:

  • All Events Report: It includes all the activities for the unit selected in the chosen period.
  • Single Event Report: It shows a single event activities for the unit selected in the chosen period.
  • Speeding Report: Shows the speeding events for the speed and unit selected in the chosen period.
  • Idles Report: It shows the events the vehicle was idle in the chosen period.
  • Vehicle Productivity: It shows mileage, usage of time and speed for a chosen period.

How to create a new user

Go to Settings module and click in “Users”. Add the new user by clicking “New” . Fill all the fields with the information of the new user and Save. You can also edit the actual information of any existent user.

How to change the name of a vehicle

Go to Settings module and click in “Vehicles”. You can edit the information of the existent vehicles by filling the fields and saving.

How to create and change Geo-fences

Go to Settings module and click in “Geo fences”. You can add a geofence or edit the information of the existent geofences by filling the fields and saving.
