How to change your password

  • Go to Settings module

  • Select the Change Password icon and follow the instructions in the pop-up window.

How to Log in

You can access the system from any computer that has Internet access. Please follow these steps:

  • Go to https://www.easitrack.net
  • You will find the Log in and Password boxes, and a button to Sign In.
  • Enter your Log in and Password and then click in Sign In.
  • After clicking Sign In you will be taken to the Mapping page. You are logged in!
