What's in this for you?

If your company uses a Fuel Card system, you can use this feature to upload this data into the system. When you upload the fuel transactions into the system:

  • We will calculate the closest odometer of the vehicle when the transaction occured.

  • The Fuel Log report will be able to show you the average MPG information per vehicle and for the entire fleet.

  • You will also see an aproximate of how many miles were driven between fueling events.

  • You'll be abe to keep track of the cost of fuel per vehicle, or for the entire fleet.

Activation is very simple:

  • Send us an email with a sample of the fuel card file you will be using to upload. Please send it via email to our Support team or to your Account Manager or Sales Rep.

  • We will homologate your file structure within the system. This process can take 1 - 3 days while our developers implement your type of file.

  • Once homologated, we will send you an email with the confirmation of activation of the feature and the easy steps to start uploading your Fuel Card data.
