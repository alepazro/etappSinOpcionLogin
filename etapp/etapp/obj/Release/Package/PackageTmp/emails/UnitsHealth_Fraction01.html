Devices with Power Cut events

The following devices are loosing power one or more times a day.

Cause: The RED wire is not connected to a constant power supply. For instance, The RED Wire could be connected to an aux line, which has no power when the vehicle is off.

Solution: Make sure that the RED wire is connected to a source that ALWAYS has power (12+ VDC, 200+ mA), even when the vehicle is OFF.
